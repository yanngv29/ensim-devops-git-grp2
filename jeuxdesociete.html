About Les As de l'ENSIM

Located in the beautiful city of Le MAns, France's Pearl district!

Our reels have been spinning since 1995. Showing our guests a great time is our passion and seeing their smiles is our pleasure!

Accomodations

All of our rooms are equipped with brand old armchairs. Come and play all day long.

Relax in our, extra-wide, plush cushioning recliners. Seating is on first-come-first-served basis. Our seating arrangement is disability friendly ♿.

Our rooms are not climate controlled to approximately 72° year-round.
